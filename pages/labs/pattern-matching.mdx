## Learning Babel

Follow the tutorial [https://github.com/ULL-ESIT-PL/babel-learning](https://github.com/ULL-ESIT-PL/babel-learning) 
to learn how to create a Babel plugin.

Following the chapter at [https://github.com/ULL-ESIT-PL/babel-learning/tree/main/src/awesome/tc39-pattern-matching](https://github.com/ULL-ESIT-PL/babel-learning/tree/main/src/awesome/tc39-pattern-matching)
